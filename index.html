Pruthwik Mishra

Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor at SVNIT, Surat in the department of Artificial Intelligence (AI). I was a post doctoral fellow at IIIT-Hyderabad working in the MeitY funded Bhasini project. I completed my PhD in NLP at IIIT-Hyderabad, under the supervision of Prof. Dipti Misra Sharma.

In my PhD, I worked on Arithmetic Word Problem Solving in English and Indian languages. I am also interested in Machine Translation, Shallow Parsing, and other NLP areas.
